About Sanjay Dutt

Sanjay Dutt, born on July 29, 1959, is one of the most celebrated and controversial actors in the Indian film industry. He is known for his versatile acting skills, and his journey in Bollywood has been filled with both triumphs and struggles. Dutt's father, Sunil Dutt, was a legendary actor, and his mother, Nargis, was an iconic actress. Sanjay Dutt made his acting debut in the 1981 film *Rocky*, directed by his father.

Throughout his career, Sanjay Dutt has starred in numerous successful films, including *Khalnayak*, *Munna Bhai MBBS*, *Lage Raho Munna Bhai*, and *Vaastav*. His role as the lovable gangster Munna Bhai in the *Munna Bhai* series is particularly iconic and loved by audiences worldwide. Despite his acting success, Sanjay Dutt has had his fair share of personal troubles, including legal issues and a period of imprisonment, which has made his life both fascinating and controversial.
